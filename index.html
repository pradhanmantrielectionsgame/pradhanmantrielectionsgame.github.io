India Elections Game - Help Guide

Game Overview

Objective

Win India's Lok Sabha Elections by securing at least 272 out of 543 seats. You compete against an AI opponent across multiple phases of campaigning.

Game Phases

The election campaign is divided into 8 phases, each lasting 30 seconds. Use your limited time and funds wisely!

Victory Conditions

  • Majority Government: Get 272+ seats to form government (evaluated at the end of all 8 phases)
  • Hung Parliament: If neither player reaches 272 at the end, it's a tie

Your Resources

  • Campaign Funds: Start with 250M, get more each phase
  • Time: Limited time per phase to make decisions
  • Strategic Planning: Choose where and how to spend wisely

Controls & Interface

Map Interaction

  • Click on States: Invest funds directly in any state or UT (single campaign)
  • Shift + Click: Burst spend - invest 3x funds at once for maximum impact
  • R + Click: Deploy rally token for popularity boost
  • State Groups: Use group buttons to target multiple states at once
  • Small UTs: Special buttons for tiny territories like Puducherry

Campaign Policies

  • Policy Cards: Right panel shows 6 policy categories
  • Investment: Click progress bars to invest in specific policies
  • Progress Tracking: Visual bars show your investment levels

Game Controls

  • Pause: Space bar or pause button to pause/resume
  • Sound: Toggle sound effects and music
  • Events: Enable/disable random campaign events
  • Help: This guide (click Help button again to close)

Information Panels

  • Player Info: Your funds and AI opponent status
  • States Info: Details about selected states
  • Actions Log: History of game events and actions
  • Game Status: Current phase, timer, seat projections
  • TV Display: Live updates and breaking news

How to Play

Building Popularity

Your goal is to increase your popularity in states to win their seats:

  • Direct Investment: Click states on the map to invest funds
  • Policy Campaigns: Invest in policies that appeal to different regions
  • Group Strategies: Target state groups for regional dominance

Seat Calculation

Seats are awarded proportionally based on popularity:

  • If you have 40% popularity in a state, you get ~40% of its seats
  • Example: Uttar Pradesh (80 seats) with 40% popularity = 32 seats
  • The AI and "Others" get the remaining seats based on their popularity

Policy Categories

  • Social Policy: Hindutva, Uniform Civil Code, Hindi mandate, Secularism
  • Justice & Inclusion: Reservations, Women's rights, Tribal rights
  • Infrastructure: Highways, Ports, Smart Cities, Defense
  • Economic Policy: MGNREGA, GST, Digital India, Industry
  • Agriculture: Farm loans, River projects, GMOs, Farm bills
  • Health & Welfare: Swachh Bharat, Ayushman, Financial inclusion

Bonus Opportunities

  • Group Domination: Get >50% in all states of a group for big bonuses
  • Policy Mastery: Max out policy investments for rewards
  • Regional Strongholds: Focus on specific regions for guaranteed wins

Strategy Tips

Early Game (Phases 1-3)

  • Secure Your Base: Focus on states where you start with an advantage
  • Broad Investment: Spread funds across multiple states initially
  • Policy Foundation: Start building key policy platforms
  • Monitor AI: Watch which states the AI is targeting

Mid Game (Phases 4-6)

  • Regional Focus: Try to dominate entire state groups
  • Contested States: Fight for swing states where you're competitive
  • Policy Synergy: Align policies with regional preferences
  • Defensive Play: Protect your strongholds from AI attacks

End Game (Phases 7-8)

  • Calculate Path to 272: Know exactly which seats you need
  • All-In Strategy: Concentrate remaining funds on winnable states
  • Block AI: Prevent AI from reaching 272 if you can't
  • Last-Minute Pushes: Use any remaining funds strategically

Regional Strategies

  • Hindi Heartland: UP, Bihar, MP - High seat count, social policies important
  • South India: TN, AP, Karnataka, Kerala - Infrastructure and economic policies
  • Coastal States: Focus on ports and maritime development
  • Northeast: Tribal rights and border security matter
  • Agricultural Regions: Farm policies and rural development

Pro Tips

  • Efficiency: Don't over-invest in states you've already secured
  • Information: Click states to see detailed popularity breakdowns
  • Timing: Save some funds for final phases when competition intensifies
  • Adaptation: Adjust strategy based on AI behavior and random events
  • Group Bonuses: Prioritize completing state groups for bonus funds
